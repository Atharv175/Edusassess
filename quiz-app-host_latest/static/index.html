About EduAssess

Transforming Teacher Education

EduAssess is a pioneering initiative launched in partnership with the PSSCIVE to revolutionize teacher training across the country. Our platform combines cutting-edge AI technology with pedagogical expertise to create a comprehensive training ecosystem.

With a focus on continuous assessment and improvement, EduAssess helps identify skill gaps and provides targeted interventions, ensuring that every teacher receives personalized support to excel in their role.

Our mission is to empower educators with the tools, resources, and insights they need to deliver high-quality education to students nationwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

🟠 How does EduAssess work?

EduAssess uses advanced AI algorithms to generate quizzes, track performance metrics, and provide real-time insights to both administrators and teachers. The platform adapts to individual learning patterns and provides personalized recommendations.

🟠 Is EduAssess free for government schools?

Yes, EduAssess is being implemented in collaboration with the PSSCIVE is provided free of cost to all government schools and teacher training institutes across the country.

🟠 Can private schools use EduAssess?

Yes, private educational institutions can subscribe to EduAssess through our partnership program. Please contact our support team for more information on pricing and implementation.

🟠 What technical requirements are needed?

EduAssess is web-based and works on any modern browser. It's accessible on computers, tablets, and smartphones with internet connectivity. No special software installation is required.
